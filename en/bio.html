caio costa campos

is a composer and improviser from Belo Horizonte, Brazil. His recent works are related to a vision of music as performance, enacting music through the lens of performance, towards a performative understanding of music to be articulated in performance far beyond that practice which happens through so-called "musical perception" or "aural skills". He has experience in creative programming, live electronics and free improvisation, both in marginal contexts and in concert environments, and bases his work on these experiences to explore these expressive intentions.

His performances have been presented in different spaces and countries, especially Brazil, the USA and China. Beyond instrumental groups and musicians, his performances also involve collaboration with other artists (and people who do not identify as artists) as performers, as is the case of facePiece (2025).

Currently based in Belo Horizonte, he holds a Master's degree in Music Composition from UT Austin and another Master's degree in Music from UFMG, in the research line Creative Analytical Processes, where he developed research entitled "Performance Notes: investigating performance composition in its relations with music processes and practices".

He also holds a technical degree in computer science from CEFET-MG, a music teaching license and a bachelor's degree in composition from UFMG.

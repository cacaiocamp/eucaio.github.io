caio costa campos

is a composer and improviser from Belo Horizonte, Brasil. His recent works are related to a view of music as performance, enacting music from performance theory lenses towards an performative comprehension of music to be articulated in performance, beyond what happens through so called "music perception" or "aural skills." He has experience with creative programming, live electronics and free improvisation in both marginal and concert settings and bases his work on those trying to achieve these performative explorations.

Currently based in Belo Horizonte, Minas Gerais, he is a Master in Composition by University of Texas at Austin, and is doing a Masters in Music at Federal University of Minas Gerais, with a research around music and composition as performance.

He has a technician degree in informatics from CEFET-MG, a music education degree and a bachelor in composition from UFMG.
